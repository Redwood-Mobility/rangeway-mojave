Why here

The Antelope Valley. 95,460 reasons.

95,460 vehicles per day on CA-14
0 premium charging options within 25 miles
48.8 acres at the Dawn Road interchange
100% off-grid solar powered

The CA-14 corridor connects the Los Angeles basin to the high desert and points north. It is the daily commute for thousands of aerospace and defense workers at Edwards Air Force Base, Northrop Grumman, Lockheed Martin, Boeing, NASA, and Virgin Galactic. On weekends, it carries travelers heading to the Eastern Sierra and beyond.

This is a technically literate, high-income workforce with above-average EV adoption. They drive this corridor every day. Right now, they have nowhere to charge.

Rangeway Mojave changes that.

Phase I

Built from the ground up. Powered by the sun.

Ultra-Fast Charging

Six DC fast charging ports delivering up to 600 kW. Pull in, plug in, and get back on the road. Every port works, every time.

100% Off-Grid Solar

Powered entirely by on-site solar generation and battery energy storage. No grid connection. No utility bills. No delays. Engineered by Carbon DC.

Food and Beverage

Fresh food and drinks served from our on-site Rivian EDV food truck. Because you deserve more than a vending machine.

Designed for the Desert

Desert-native landscaping, shaded seating areas, and a site designed with the same care as the charging experience. Premium from the pavement up.

Phase II brings the full Rangeway experience: a 2,500 square foot climate-controlled driver's lounge with a staffed cafe, comfortable seating, premium restrooms, and on-site coffee roasting from Red Canyon Roasting Company. The lounge is coming shortly after launch.

How it works

Sunlight in. Electrons out. Grid optional.

/01

Solar panels generate the power.

A 990 kW ground-mounted solar array captures Mojave sunlight across several acres of dedicated solar field. This is one of the strongest solar-resource areas in the United States.

/02

Batteries store it.

A 3.6 MWh battery energy storage system holds the energy and manages load, ensuring consistent charging power regardless of time of day or cloud cover.

/03

Chargers deliver it.

Ultra-fast DC charging ports dispense the stored solar energy directly to your vehicle. The entire system operates independently of the electrical grid.

This is not experimental technology. Carbon DC's off-grid charging systems are already operating at multiple sites across California's desert corridors. We are deploying a proven platform, not testing a prototype.

Why Rangeway

The stop should be worth the time.

We think like hotel operators, not utility companies. The stop should be worth the time.

Rangeway is America's first hospitality-driven premium EV charging network. We are not building more chargers in more parking lots. We are building destinations where the stop itself becomes part of the journey.

Every Rangeway location is designed from the perspective of the person who just got out of the car. What do they need? What would make them feel welcome? What would make them want to come back?

Rangeway Mojave is the first location in a planned network along America's highways, focused on routes where drivers need reliable charging and comfortable stops during extended travel. Every network starts with one site. This is ours.

Zak Winnick, Founder and CEO
